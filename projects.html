Places I Have Been


Mexico

I have been to Mexico once and the things I liked most about it was the food and the resort.

Puerto Rico

I have been to Puerto Rico once and the things I enjoyed most was the food and the beautiful beach in San Juan.

Toronto, Canada

I've been to Toronto many times and my favorite things to do are go to Blue Jays games, Raptors games, and enjoy exploring the downtown parts. The only thing I don't like about it is that the traffic is usually horrible.

Las Vegas

I've only been to Las Vegas once and it is quite the experience. The things I enjoyed most was the food and the sportsbooks they have. I am not too much of a gambler so I did not go crazy and lose a ton of money like most people.
